Danish

[edit]
\n

Etymology

[edit]
\n

From Middle Low German orden, from Latin \u014drd\u014d (\u201corder, rank\u201d). Doublet of ordre.\n

\n

Pronunciation

[edit]
\n
  • IPA(key): /\u0254r\u02c0d\u0259n/, [\u02c8\u0252\u02c0d\u0325n\u0329]
\n

Noun

[edit]
\n

orden c (singular definite ordenen or (unofficial) ordnen, plural indefinite ordener or ordner)\n

\n
  1. order, neatness (proper arrangement of things)\n
    Antonyms: rod, uorden
    2. \n
  2. order (arrangement, disposition, sequence of things)
    3. \n
  3. order (level in a hierarchy)
    4. \n
  4. (taxonomy) order (taxonomical classification)
    5. \n
  5. order (religious group or a society of knights)
    6. \n
  6. decoration, order (awarded decoration)

Noun

[edit]
\n

orden f (plural \u00f3rdenes)\n

\n
  1. order (command)\n
    Es una orden, no una pregunta
    This is an order, not a question
    \n
    A sus \u00f3rdenes \u2015 How may I help you? (literally, \u201cAt your command\u201d)
    2. \n
  2. order (request, purchase, or food)
    3. \n
  3. order (law)\n
    • 2025 November 26, \u201cJuez federal dice que los oficiales de inmigraci\u00f3n en Colorado solo pueden arrestar a quienes corren el riesgo de huir\u201d, in CNN en Espa\u00f1ol‎[1], sourced from Associated Press:
      Un juez federal dictamin\u00f3 el martes que los oficiales de inmigraci\u00f3n en Colorado solo pueden arrestar a personas sin una orden judicial si creen que es probable que esas personas huyan.
      (please add an English translation of this quotation)
    4. \n
  4. order (decoration)\n
    La Orden del Tois\u00f3n de Oro (the highest decoration of Spain) \u2015 The Order of the Golden Fleece
    5. \n
  5. (military) order
    6. \n
  6. (religion) order\n
    entr\u00f3 a una nueva orden religiosa
    (someone) joined a new religious order
\n

Noun

[edit]

    Est\u00e1n en orden alfab\u00e9tico
    They are in alphabetical order
    2. \n
  2. orderliness
    3. \n
  3. good behavior
    4. \n
  4. (taxonomy) order
    5. \n
  5. (mathematics) order (highest exponent)
